Getting Inspired by the Problem

The beauty of the web is that it continiously evolves every day. That's why I get inspired by problems and the people that face them. It gives me the motivation to create solutions and continue working to enhance and give the users an unforgettable experience. Here are some of the case studies from work that I've done.

  • Gamer's Hive is a networking platform for gamers who are looking to find others at their same skill level to play together or build a gaming team.

  • Mesothelioma Guide is a one stop shop for patients and family membeers to get information about the disease, as well as medical and financial help.

  • AddictionCenter helps family members and patients sufferring from addiction find a place to get treated and get back on-track with their lives.
